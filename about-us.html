Cellula – Code of conduct statement

Cellula and domain www.thecellula.com is owned by Coshika Enterprise Life Laboratory with its registered office in Bangalore, India, Cellula is a digital healthcare service provider with a progressive approach. We aspire to serve the global market with our extensive and deep expertise. Partnerships with service providers and suppliers are core to our business. Next to this, our 7 core values, Customer centricity, Empowerment, Leadership, Learning, Utility, Local and Accountability are essential elements of all we do. In this way, we aspire to build up a strong reputation with an extensive customer portfolio, global reach, unmatched quality and commitment to improving quality of life.

While executing our business we shall stay committed to following 7 guiding principals

  • Data Security is our utmost priority
  • Ethical Business practice is core to our business
  • Compliance to law and regulation is our commitment
  • Environment protection and adopting environment friendly business practices is our fundamental
  • Interest of customer, employee, partners and suppliers interest is considered prime
  • We encourage equal opportunity principal and discourage harassment, discrimination and bullying
  • Safety of people, employee, places and environment is our duty
